Checklist

This view provides the means to add categorized checklist groups that contain checklist items.  Checklist items are manually entered only once. Thereafter, the item can quickly be added. A simple checkmark is used to denote when a checklist item has been completed.  For example, the Scuba Gear checklist group has a checkmark beside it. This denotes that items in that group such as mask, fin, snorkel, regulator, etc has been packed and is ready-to-go.
