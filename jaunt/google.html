Google Search Engine

This view performs Google search engine queries for each trip destination.  When a selection is tapped, such as Hotels, it will display the list of Hotels found sorted nearest to your location. Further tapping reveals a full detailed description, prices, distance to the Hotel, website, and turn-by-turn directions to the Hotel.
